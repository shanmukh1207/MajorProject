connect-flash npm..


The flash is a special area of the session used for storing messages. Messages are written in the flash and cleared after being displayed to the user. General appears for once something in a form of alert like whenever we delete something whenever they add something.

it is also a middleware

npm i connect-flash

app.use(flash())

we pass two things key and message

app.use(flash());


app.get("/register",(req,res)=>{
    let {name  = "anonaymus"} = req,query;
    req.session.name = name;
    req.flash("success","user registered successfully!");
    res.reirect("/hello");

});

app.get("/hello",(req,res)=>{
res.send(`/hello",${req.session.name,msg: req.flash("success")}`);
});
